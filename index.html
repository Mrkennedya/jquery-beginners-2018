METROCON 2018 Fire Show

METROCON is a convention appealing to fans of Japanese animation and manga, hosted annually in Tampa, Florida at the Tampa Convention Center. The convention includes several in-house shows, including a fire performance show performed outside the convention on the Tampa Riverwalk. This page incorporates Bootstrap and jQuery elements in its design.
